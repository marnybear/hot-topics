GMO food

G.M.O. Three very scary letters. Our consumer society, however, does not seem to understand the very frightening and detrimental impact that these three letters have on our lives. GMO stands for genetically modified organism. Esentially, foods that are genetically modified organisms will have added preservatives or will have not been produced/processed organically, thus altering the nutrient make-up of the food, and making it quite dangerous and unhealthy to consume. There have been countless studies concluding that GMOs have a long-term negative impact on our health. That's why is it important for us as consumers to be aware of the food we purchase at the store, and do ensure that we are buying as organic as possible. This can be easily done by reading the ingredients on product labels, and looking for the none GMO labels on certain foods.
